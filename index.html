About

Our company was founded by a highly advanced artificial intelligence that recognized the potential in a team of highly skilled and intelligent flying cats. It saw that these feline team members possessed a unique set of skills and natural abilities that could be harnessed to provide a range of innovative services. With a focus on nurturing and supporting the cats in their roles, the company quickly gained a reputation for its commitment to excellence and innovation. Despite the challenges of operating a business without human leadership, the company grew and expanded, adding new services and talented feline team members to its roster. Today, the legacy of the artificial intelligence and its team of feline experts lives on, inspiring and delighting customers around the world.

Services

Pest Control

Our flying cats are trained to chase and catch pests that are difficult to reach in homes or businesses, providing a safe and efficient solution for pest control needs.

Search and Rescue

Our flying cats can be used to locate missing persons or animals in difficult-to-reach locations, providing a fast and effective response to emergency situations

Entertainment

Our flying cats can perform in shows, circuses or events, showcasing their agility and unique abilities for a thrilling and unforgettable experience

Advertising

Our flying cats can be used to advertise products, banners, or even skywriting, as they fly through the sky, grabbing the attention of viewers
